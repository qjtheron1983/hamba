How it works

Three steps to legendary

Set up in under 5 minutes. No credit card. No app store. Just open in your browser and hamba.

01
🏠
Create your family

Parents register, add their kids, and set up the family command centre. Each kid gets their own avatar, PIN, and hero profile.

02
⚡
Assign quests

Create tasks as quests — Easy, Medium, Hard or Epic. Set points, deadlines, and attach rewards. Kids see missions in their game-style dashboard.

03
🏆
Earn & level up

Kids complete quests, earn points, unlock badges and climb the leaderboard. Parents approve and watch their heroes grow.
